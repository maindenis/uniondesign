Prymary logo

the logo = Symbol + logotype

The Logo has two components - a Symbol with three abstracts children’s figures against the background of the sun, and the Logo that uses special fonts that are not allowed to be replaced by any others.

Let the logo breathe

We respect the Logo by giving it some space. The minimum clear space that must surround the Logo is equivalent to the height of its capital S.

minimum size

In print, the Logo should never appear
smaller than 1” (25mm).

On screen, it must appear at least 70 pixels wide.
